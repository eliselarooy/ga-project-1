REVERSI

Reversi, sometimes known as Othello is a strategy board game for two players.

Each player takes turns in laying a counter on the board. The player's disc is laid in line with another of their counters, with their opponents counters in between. All the opponents counters in between the player's counters are turned to the their colour.

Once all the squares have been filled, or neither player can make a move, the player with the most pieces with their colour face up wins.
